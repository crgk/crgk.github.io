---
title: Mob Programming 
---

_Part of a series of learnings from <http://lambdaconf.us/>_

> All the people working together on the same thing, at the same computer.

In short, mob programming features 3-5 developers swarming together, all the time.
One person "drives" at the keyboard, another person "navigates", and the others
offer insight and devote some resources to thinking about related aspects of
the problem.
