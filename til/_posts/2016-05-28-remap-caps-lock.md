---
title: Remap Caps Lock on Mac OS X
---

In Mac OS X, you can remap the Caps Lock key to any of the
modifier keys.

System Preferences > Keyboard > Keyboard Tab > Modifier Keys... button

![modifier-keys-dialog](/img/modifier-keys-dialog.jpg)

Because when was the last time you actually used Caps Lock?

BONUS FEATURE: 

Some early keyboards had the Control key where we're used to having the Caps Lock key:
<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Caps_lock#Caps_Lock_versus_Control_key>
